Salmon Cobb Salad

Description

  • 1 head Bibb lettuce, leaves separated
  • 8 ounces cooked bacon, cut into pieces
  • 1 avocado, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
  • 3 hard-boiled eggs, quartered and halved
  • 1 (3 ounce) package smoked salmon
  • ground black pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Whisk buttermilk, mayonnaise, shallot, dill, lemon juice, and salt together in a
    bowl until dressing is smooth; refrigerate.
  2. Mix lettuce, bacon, avocado, eggs, and salmon together in a bowl. Add dressing
    and toss until coated; season with black pepper.
